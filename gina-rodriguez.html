Who is Gina Rodriguez?

Gina Rodriguez is an American actress, singer, social media personality, and Philanthropist. She is best known for her main role as Jane Villanueva in the CW satirical romantic dramedy series Jane the Virgin (2014-2019) for which she won a Golden Globe Award in 2015 where she gained much fame and respect.

In addition, Gina’s outstanding acting abilities are listed in Hollywood Reporter named one of the top 35 Latinos under 35, and referred to her as the “next big thing.” Furthermore, she was listed as one of the top 100 influential people worldwide by Time magazine.

As a singer, Gina won over her fans when she performed Majo Tenorio in the film Filly Brown which proved to be a turning point in her career. Additionally, she has been involved with various charity activities.

Later, Rodriguez appeared in many movies including Deepwater Horizon (2016), Ferdinand (2017), Annihilation (2018), Miss Bela (2019), Someone Great (2019), Scoob (2020), Awake (2021), I Want You Back (2022), Spy Kids: Armageddon (2023).
