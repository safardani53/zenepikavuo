Ellen Pompeo Joins Taylor Swift Music Video: Check Out Her Poster!

 Posted on August 20, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  174 words  |  Martina Birk

Ellen Pompeo has a special connection to Taylor Swift.

The singer actually named one of her cats after the Grey’s Anatomy star, with Pompeo making Swift’s life on two occasions:

  • She and Mariska Hargitay (after whom Swift named her other cat) sent a picture to their biggest fan on Super Bowl Sunday this year.
  • Pompeo jokingly offered Swift some medical advice after Taylor’s feline scratched her legs.

    • So we can only now imagine how Swift’s head is exploding upon the news that Pompeo will play a role in the artist’s Bad Blood music video.

    Swift was the one who shared this news with Twitter followers today, sending out the above poster and writing as a caption to it:

    "Meet Luna. @EllenPompeo #BadBloodMusicVideo."

    The Bad Blood music video will debut as the opening to this Sunday’s Billboard Music Awards on ABC and we already know if will feature such stars as Lena Dunham, Gigi Hadid and Jessica Alba.
