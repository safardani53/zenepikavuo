THE best walking trousers are an essential for those looking to go on long walks or hikes.

Whether you are an experienced walker looking to update your wardrobe, or a beginner in need of the basic outerwear, we have just the thing.

When shopping for walking trousers the main elements to look out for are designs that are waterproof and insulated.

Designs which are moisture-wicking is an added bonus as they help to regulate the temperature of the body and draw sweat away from the skin so not to leave you feeling damp, cold and sweaty on your walk.

Walking trousers come in different lengths to suit those who want longer length or capri designs if they feel restricted in a full trouser.

Whatever your style preference, we have found the best buys to suit your needs, and budget.
