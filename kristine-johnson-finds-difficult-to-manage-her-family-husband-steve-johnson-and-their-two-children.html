Kristine Johnson finds difficult to manage her family- husband Steve Johnson and their two children

 Posted on September 30, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  196 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

This is what Kristie Johnson had to say when asked about the task of managing her personal and private affairs. In this competitive journalism field, having the nickname as is quite an accomplishment. And such a nickname cannot be achieved without making some grave compromises.

Maybe all this would not have been possible if her husband wasn’t supportive enough. She has been married to Steve Johnson for quite some time now. The couple has two children together, a daughter and a younger son.

Having to work from 4pm to 11pm, she said how hard it was to spend quality time with her family. Her work demands a no-break policy. She gets to sleep a maximum of 5 hours because she has to get up early to prepare her children for school. She could obviously ask her husband to cook the meals so she can get a few more hours of sleep. But that would mean sacrificing time together with her children. Focusing a little more on your career than your family might be an invitation to divorce as has been the case with many so far. So Kristie has learned the hard way to make things work.
