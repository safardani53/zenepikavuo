RHOBH fans think Diana Jenkins dropped clue she's been fired from show during fiery reunion special

 Posted on June 18, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  469 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

THE REAL Housewives of Beverly Hills fans think that Diana Jenkins may have already been fired from the cast.

The Bravo show’s newest star was absent from her first ever reunion tonight.

During the first part of the RHOBH season 12 reunion, Sutton Stracke, Garcelle Beauvais, Kyle Richards, Lisa Rinna, Erika Jayne, Dorit Kemsley, and Crystal Kung Minkoff sat down with host Andy Cohen.

However, the Bravo boss explained: “As you can see by the empty spot on the sofa, we are one Housewife short today.

“Diana is not feeling well and we hope to be checking in with her from home later in the show.”

But the Room 23 author’s decision to not attend her first ever reunion in-person sparked theories from the show’s avid fans.

One on social media wrote: “I think she’s gonna get fired, and this kind of hints to that.”

“How can Diana miss the reunion and not get fired?” asked another.

A third raged: “Diana is a horrendous flop. Housewives like her need to be fired and forgotten."

Season 12 has been Diana's first shot at reality TV, but she's found herself on the wrong side of many viewers who have slammed her as out of touch.

The Bosnia-native has also isolated herself from the rest of the cast by staying in separate hotels during girls' trips, while quickly making enemies with Sutton and Garcelle.

Last month, she appeared to hint that her time as a Housewife may be up as she posted to Instagram: "Sunsets are proof that endings can be beautiful too."

Although she was not at the reunion in person, Diana did appear via video at the end of the episode.

'HOW EVIL?'

Host Andy put Diana under the spotlight following the recent racist online attacks against Garcelle's 14-year-old son Jax.

"There are a lot of people right now accusing you of being behind the bots that went after Garcelle’s 14-year-old."

He asked Garcelle: "Was there a point that you thought she was behind it?"

"Yeah I did think she was behind it, absolutely," she replied.

But Diana was keen to claim her innocence: "I don’t know how evil I must appear that you would even consider the fact that I would do that to Jax."

UNDER INVESTIGATIONS

Since the reunion was filmed, Diana has filed a legal investigation to discover who was behind the cyberbullying bots.

Her lawsuit states that her goal is to “unmask and hold accountable the morally bankrupt person who has attacked a child and placed blame for his/her actions on Ms. Jenkins.”
