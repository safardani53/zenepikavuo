KHLOE Kardashian has divided opinion she went on a Twitter spree late Wednesday night and made some rather cheeky comments.

Mom-of-two Khloe couldn't resist replying to some of her followers and even referred to one of them as "daddy".

One person tweeted: "Not Kris making the guy make peacock sounds #TheKardashians," and Khloe retweeted it and joked: "She's an a**hole."

And when a different Twitter user asked: "can i suck your toes," Khloe shared the tweet and added: "Yes daddy."

In another tweet she asked her 30.4 million followers: "Who wants to moots me," and later added: "Moots daddy. Moots me."
