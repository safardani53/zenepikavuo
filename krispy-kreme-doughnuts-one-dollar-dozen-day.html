How do you get the $1 dozen doughnuts at Krispy Kreme?

 Posted on February 16, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  502 words  |  Martina Birk

KRISPY Kreme is bringing back its Day of the Dozens on Tuesday, December 12, 2023.

Now, customers want to know more about the limited-time holiday offer.

What is Krispy Kreme’s Day of the Dozens offer?

Krispy Kreme's Day of the Dozens is an annual event that is taking place on Tuesday, December 12, 2023, as the company announced via a December 7 news release.

Customers can get one dozen of the bakery chain's Original Glazed doughnuts for only $1.

Krispy Kreme stated: "On 12/12 only, Krispy Kreme fans can treat themselves, loved ones, friends and coworkers to this sweet holiday deal in-shop or drive-thru for pick-up at participating shops, or by ordering online for pickup or delivery."

How do you get a dozen Krispy Kreme doughnuts for one dollar?

You can get a dozen Krispy Kreme Original Glazed doughnuts for $1 by purchasing any dozen or a 16-count of Minis at regular price, per the brand's news release.

It continues: "Krispy Kreme’s Day of the Dozens offer is limited to two redemptions for guests who purchase in-shop or via drive-thru and one redemption for guests who purchase online for pick-up or delivery using promo code 'DOZEN.'"

What other holiday offerings does Krispy Kreme have?

According to Krispy Kreme's holiday promotions page, it is offering multiple seasonal deals that began on November 24 and will continue through December 24, 2023.

The company is making customers Elf-themed doughnuts in honor of the hit Christmas film's 20th anniversary.

Its Day of the Dozens news release states: "Krispy Kreme also continues to celebrate the season with its 'Elf' Holiday Collection, available in a limited-edition 'Elf'-themed dozen box in celebration of the 20th anniversary of the family-favorite holiday classic."

The company announced the offer in a November 20, 2023 news release, revealing that the Elf doughnuts include three new festive flavors and one returning holiday menu item.

The new flavors include:

  • Buddy Snow Globe Doughnut - "Original Glazed Doughnut dipped in sugar cookie light blue icing and white sprinkles, topped with powdered sugary snow and a chocolate 'Elf' piece."
  • Buddy Makes Breakfast Doughnut - "Original Glazed Doughnut topped with cake batter spaghetti buttercreme, milk chocolate colorful candies, sprinkles, and a maple drizzle."
  • Christmas Lights Doughnut - "Original Glazed Doughnut dipped in rich chocolate icing, drizzled with green icing, and topped with rainbow sprinkles and a chocolate 'Elf' piece."

The Santa Belly Doughnut is also part of the offer.

The returning sweet treat is "filled with Cookies & Kreme filling, dipped in red icing and decorated with Santa’s belt and belt buckle candy piece."

Per the chain's holiday promotions page, customers can also purchase holiday beverages including the Sugar Cookie Latte and the Ghirardelli Hot Chocolate.

The Sugar Cookie Latte is served iced, hot, or frozen and is "a delicious espresso-based latte blended with sugar cookie flavored syrup topped with whipped cream and sprinkle blend."

The Ghirardelli Hot Chocolate contains "Ghirardelli cocoa blended with steamed milk and topped with whipped cream and a chocolate drizzle."
