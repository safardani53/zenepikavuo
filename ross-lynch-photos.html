Ross Lynch Photos Of The Actor Hollywood Life

 Posted on July 1, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  174 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Ross rose to fame playing Austin Moon on the hit Disney Channel series Austin & Ally. He went on to star as Brady in the Teen Beach Movie series. The actor/singer is also one of the founding members of R5. The band is made up of Ross and his brothers Riker Lynch, Rocky Lynch, and their sister Rydel Lynch, as well as their family friend Ellington Ratliff. After branching out from his roles on the Disney Channel, Ross starred as the young version of serial killer Jeffrey Dahmer in the film, My Friend Dahmer. It focused on the murderer’s life when he was in high school, and premiered at the Tribeca Film Festival in April 2017. He has most recently starred as Sabrina Spellman’s love interest in The Sabrina the Teenage Witch Netflix reboot.  Netflix describes his character Harvey as “the prince charming of this dark fairy tale, Harvey is the son of a coal miner, a dreamboat and a dreamer, completely unaware of the dark forces conspiring to keep him and Sabrina apart.” Scroll through the gallery above to see more pics of Ross over the years!
