IT can be tricky trying to find a pair of trousers smart enough to wear for work that don’t dig in round the waist or restrict your movement. 

But one savvy shopper has found the perfect pair that both look uber stylish and “feel like joggers” – the perfect combination of formality and comfort. 

Priya is a petite fashion influencer who regularly shares her top finds with her 44k followers on her TikTok page @PriyaCoco.

And in a recent video, she raved about a pair of Uniqlo trousers that are perfect “if you want to dress comfortable but also look put together”. 

“I’m literally obsessed with these trousers,” the fashionista told fans as she modelled the pair of black tailored pants that look perfect for a day at the office. 

The exact pair in question are Uniqlo’s Dry Straight Leg Joggers, which look like formal trousers from the outside, but have the same drawstring stretchy waist as a pair of comfies.

They come in cream, brown and black, and cost a very reasonable £24.90 – although some sizes are out out stock, so you might have to set up alerts to get notified as they go back on sale. 

Priya clearly loves the comfy pants, adding “I bloat during the day, so I just want space round my stomach” as she showed off the loose waistband. 

She stepped back to show off the gorgeous trousers, adding that despite only being 5ft she “hasn’t had to tailor them at all”. 

She captioned the clip, which racked up a huge 327k views, by simply calling them “my favourite trousers”.
