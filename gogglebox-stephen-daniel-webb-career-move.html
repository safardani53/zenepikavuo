Gogglebox stars Stephen and Daniel Webb reveal huge new career moves after announcing show departure

 Posted on September 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  376 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

GOGGLEBOX stars Stephen and Daniel Webb have revealed a huge new career move after announcing their show departure.

The pair, who quit the Channel 4 show last week, are launching a second hair salon in West Sussex.

When Stephen, 50, and Daniel, 43, weren't filming for the popular Channel 4 show, the couple worked as hairdressers for their 9-5s.

Now they will be able to put all their time into their their new salon - Lustig and Webb Hairdressing - which is based in Storrington.

They also own Lustig and Webb which is located in the village of Hurstpierpoint.

Brighton coffee shop Puzzlebored shared a snap of the pair in their cafe with a caption talking about their plans.

The new salon already boasts 888 followers on Instagram.

Last week the couple shocked fans when they revealed that they are standing down from the show after 10 years.

They said in a joint statement:

"After careful consideration, Daniel and I have decided we will not be returning for the new series of Gogglebox.

"We feel the time is right to move on and explore other opportunities.

"We would both like to thank Channel 4 and Studio Lambert for letting us be part of this amazing, funny and heartwarming show.

"We are also extremely thankful to the cast and crew, or Gogglebox family as we call them and of course the fans, thank you for all of your love & support over the years."

Stephen has been on Gogglebox since the first series in 2013.
