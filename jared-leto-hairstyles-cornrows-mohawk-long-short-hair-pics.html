Jared Leto’s Hairstyles: Cornrows For ‘Panic Room’

Jared sported some cool cornrows while starring in the 2002 thriller film, Panic Room. For someone who loves to experiment with tons of crazy hairstyles, we’re shocked this ‘do didn’t last much longer than the duration of the film!

Jared Leto Cuts Off Gorgeous Ombre Locks For ‘Suicide Squad’

Jared Leto’s Pink Mohawk

The Thirty Seconds To Mars frontman sure got grungy when he sported a pink mohawk in 2013. He had just finished filming Dallas Buyers Club where he played a transgender character, and then switched to a classic rock ‘n’ roll look.

“It’s a center-of-attention haircut. It was kind of a f– you and silly at the same time. Nobody took it very seriously, including myself,” Jared Rolling Stone in November 2013.

Jared Leto Plans To Make His Joker In ‘Suicide Squad’ The Best One Yet

Jared Leto’s Long Ombre Hair

We’re already missing Jared’s long, luscious ombre locks! The famous hair that dominated the 2014 awards season even gave a lot of the actresses a run for their money! His perfect waves always seemed to be on point no matter the occasion.

Jared Leto’s Haircut

Jared’s new 2015 ‘do seems so normal compared to the other adventurous styles he rocked! But on March 2, Suicide Squad writer David Ayer teased Jared’s haircut, right before posting a clean-shaven Jared — which we’re still not used to!
