Can I withdraw my free spins winnings? 

In order to withdraw any winnings from these free spins, you may be required to meet a wagering requirement.

For instance, players might have to wager 10 x the winnings from the free spins bonus, before any winnings can be withdrawn.

This means that if you win £10 through your free spins, you’d have to wager £100 (10 x 10) before you could withdraw the £10, to meet the wagering requirement.  

You might also find that some bingo sites place a cap on winnings gained via free spins. 

So, it is possible to win real money on a free spins AND withdraw that money, provided that you still have accessible funds in your player’s account once the wagering requirement has been met.
