Angelina Jolie Engaged: Wedding Dress Ideas

 Posted on April 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  219 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Courtesy: Badgley Mischka; Monique Lhuillier; Reem Acra; Inset: AP

What is one of the world’s most-watched women — Angelina Jolie — going to wear on her wedding day? Something absolutely spectacular, if these bridal designers have anything to do with it!

Mark Badgley and James Mischka submitted a sketch of a classic, vintage-inspired gown (left) to PEOPLE. “[Something] like this would be perfect for Angelina,” they say. “Its beaded bodice would give her just the right amount of sparkle, and the fitted tulle skirt with godets makes it elegant and totally glamorous.”

The ever-romantic Monique Lhuillier had a different vision, sketching a Chantilly lace gown with a beautiful V-neckline, low back and high slit (center). “Angie has style,” she tells PEOPLE. “She takes a classic look and adds a sultry, sexy edge and makes it her own.”

Perhaps that classic look is what Reem Acra went for, then; the Lebanese designer says she envisions Jolie in a body-skimming ivory satin gown complete with a dramatic embellishment at the shoulder (right).

Any dress Jolie wears to walk down the aisle will likely be lovely (and maybe show a little leg), but we want to hear what you think. Tell us: Which sketch do you like best? How do you envision Jolie on her wedding day?
