PREGNANT Daily Pop host Morgan Stewart flipped the middle finger, multiple times, at co-host Justin Sylvester on live TV after he made several snide remarks.

He commented on her relationship with Dr. Phil’s son, Jordan McGraw, as well as her extravagant lifestyle.

The hosts started out talking about Kelly Rowland announcing she’s pregnant with her second child while also revealing she was hesitant about sharing the happy news.

This led to Justin asking fellow host Morgan if she also felt nervous to share all of her happy news – her recent engagement to Jordan and their baby news – amid the coronavirus pandemic and with so many dealing with hardships.

She explained to him, as well as to viewers on the live pop culture program: “I wasn’t hesitant to share the engagement or the pregnancy, those were two things I was really excited about.

“But just sort of my day-to-day life and other things that might seem a little bit more flamboyant, and I understand I-“

Morgan, 32, was cut off when Justin jumped in with a snide comment about how well-off she is, saying under his breath: “Like your house.”

His co-host stumbled a little bit and said, “Thank you,” before proceeding to slyly flip him off with one middle finger scratching her cheek.

She started to continued with what she was saying beforehand about knowing she’s “in an extremely privileged position” when Justin again jumped in to say, “Like your new pool.”

This earned the 33-year-old the double middle finger, with Morgan now scratching her cheeks with both of them, while adding that she’s been “a ittle bit more cognizant of” her privilege “because I don’t want to jam that down people’s throats.” 

To top it all of, Justin decided to get one more remark in when Morgan started to say she’s “aware” not everyone is doing as well as she is right now and has all that she has.

“Like your hot fiance,” he said of what else she has going for her right now.

While she agreed she has so many good things this year, Morgan shared that feels like everyone else’s 2020 was her 2019. 

She added, while laughing at all of the comments her co-host made: “That year took a hot dumpster fire on my face, so this is why I got an easier 2020.”

He agreed with her, also laughing: “That’s true, that’s true."

The two seemed to make light of her flipping him off and of all of his underhand comments, smiling and laughing it all off.

They both even reshared social media posts about their interaction on live TV, with fans calling it “EVERYTHING!!”

Morgan referenced her difficult 2019 during the program, referring to splitting from her ex-husband Brendan Fitzpatrick.

She filed for divorce at the Stanley Mosk Courthouse in Los Angeles, California last October, and the two finalized the end of their marriage this past July 30.

The former couple tied the knot on E!’s Rich Kids of Beverly Hills and were married for three years.

The Sun exclusively reported that Morgan begged a Los Angeles judge to speed up her divorce from Brendan, 31, after she became engaged to McGraw.

The parties filed a declaration for an uncontested dissolution on June 11, 2020.

Los Angeles Superior Court documents shared that Morgan and Brendan "entered into a written agreement regarding their property and their marriage," including spousal support and attorney fees.

The exes waived their right to trial and to appeal the divorce judgment.

In the paperwork, the parties requested the court “approve” the written agreement.

Brendan has been rumored to be dating Bachelor alumna Amanda Stanton after the two were spotted together in LA following his split with Morgan.

The Daily Pop host announced in August that she and her fiance Jordan were expecting their first child together, just one month after revealing their engagement on July 4.

She and Dr. Phil's son had only been dating for a few months when he decided to put a ring on it, but the talk show host revealed they had actually first dated a decade ago.

She explained: "We dated 10 years ago for a year and then he broke up because he was like, ‘I’m not down with you, b***h.’

"And then I was like ‘Fine, whatever, I’m over you, I don’t care.’ And then I went on obviously to have another relationship.

"Then the past year we got back together. Isn’t that cute? He was persistent and I was like, ‘all right.'"

Dr. Phil congratulated the happy couple on Morgan's pregnancy.
