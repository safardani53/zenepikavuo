M&amp;S are selling a 35 knitted jumper that shoppers say is the buy of the autumn its so warm

 Posted on February 28, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  335 words  |  Martina Birk

M&S fans have already hailed the store’s fluffy cream shoulder bag as the must-have accessory of this autumn. 

And it now seems that the retailer has also taken the crown for the best knitted jumper, with fans dashing to pick up a £35 piece that influencers are hailing as the ‘perfect’ product. 

Stunning lifestyle influencer Caitlin Mazza shared the find with her 21.6k TikTok followers, in a clip that amassed a massive 63.4k views. 

Showing off the jumper, the fashionista declared “I have found the most aesthetic sweater from M&S!”

The item in question is the store’s Fair Isle Crew Neck Jumper, currently on sale for £35. 

On M&S’s website, the sweater is described as a “bold yet timeless piece”, finished with “ribbed trims on the neck, cuffs and hem”.
