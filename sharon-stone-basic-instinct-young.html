What has she said about the Basic Instinct Leg cross scene?

Sharon's character Catherine Tramell uncrosses her legs without wearing underwear several times during a police interrogation in the 1992 thriller.

Marty allegedly told Sharon that the film could not be shown in line with the Screen Actors Guild.

"It wasn't legal to shoot up my dress in this fashion," she wrote.

"I let Paul know of the options Marty had laid out for me. Of course, he vehemently denied that I had any choices at all. I was just an actress, just a woman; what choices could I have?

"But I did have choices. So I thought and thought and I chose to allow this scene in the film. Why? Because it was correct for the film and for the character; and because, after all, I did it."

Paul, 82, denied Sharon's allegations back in 2017, claiming: "Sharon is lying.
