TEEN Mom Cheyenne Floyd indulged in her "weird" pregnancy cravings with Hot Cheetos and pickles after revealing she's expecting her second child.

The MTV star is pregnant with her second child, but her first with boyfriend Zach Davis.

Cheyenne, 28, shared the bizarre combination on her Instagram account.

She posted a shot of a large pickle and Hot Cheeto bag and wrote: "These pregnancy cravings are getting weirder and weirder."

Cheyenne then shared a Boomerang of herself eating the pickle with a Hot Cheeto on top and wrote: "Life is great."

After weeks of speculation, Cheyenne confirmed that she is expecting her second child on Instagram.

She wrote: “We are extremely blessed and honored that this little one has chosen us as parents. 

“We prayed for this moment and wanted to hold on to it as long as we could. The past few months have been life changing in the best ways possible. 

“Ryder is so excited to be a big sister again and is already super protective over my bump.

"She prays for the baby every night and kisses my belly every morning." 

The Teen Mom OG star then went to thank her boyfriend and wrote: “Zach, you have been nothing but supportive and there every step of the way. 

“Thinking about how young we were when we first met and how far we have come, I could not be more proud of us today. My heart is so full. Let the next adventure began.”

The 26-year-old also referenced the pregnancy “secret” she was accused of after being flooded with baby speculation in her latest social media posts.

She said: “Our pregnancy was never some huge secret like many of you have assumed and exposed over and over again. 

"Many women wait for their first trimester before they announce. I showed a lot quicker this pregnancy. 

“I have been getting asked and told I was pregnant for the past two years. Any sign of weight gain and I was being told I was pregnant.”

The Los Angeles native then asked her followers to “do better” and “respect women."

The speculation intensified after Cheyenne seemed to make a baby registry, which was later deleted.

The Buy Buy Baby registry was screengrabbed, however, and showed the couple's gift ideas for the baby - allegedly due in June 2021.

Then, the Teen Mom star ignited pregnancy rumors once again when she appeared to "hide her belly" while having some fun in a dinosaur costume.

Back in October, she was seen sipping on sparkling juice at her 28th birthday bash - which only led fans to assume she is expecting.

At the time, one commenter wrote: "I thought she looked pregnant too," while another confidently added: "She pregnant."

Her first baby daddy and ex, Cory Wharton, congratulated her on the big news, and commented: “Finally!! Dear Lord YES CHEY IS PREGNANT!!!!! 

“Congratulations Chey and Zach happy for you both! And Ryder you’re gonna have another sibling!!!!”

Cory's baby mama, Taylor Selfridge, also showed her love with a line of hearts and wrote "Yayyyy finally!!"
