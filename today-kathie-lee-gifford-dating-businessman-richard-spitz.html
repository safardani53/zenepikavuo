TRAGIC LOVE LOSS

Kathie Lee was married to NFL star, Frank Gifford from 1986 until his death in 2015.

The football star battled concussion-related brain disease, CTE (chronic traumatic encephalopathy).

They shared two children, Cody and Cassidy.

Frank reportedly left his entire estate to Kathy Lee which included $10million worth of liquid assets, a $2.5million house in Florida, and a $22million mansion in Connecticut.

Before Frank, Kathie Lee was married to Christian composer Paul Johnson, whom she divorced in 1983.

KATHIE LEE'S CAREER

Kathie Lee is best known for co-hosting ABC's Live! with Regis and Kathie Lee from 1985 to 2000, alongside the late Regis Philbin.

In 2001, Kelly Ripa took over her position.
