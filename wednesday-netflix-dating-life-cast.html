'Wednesday' Cast's Dating Life And Bio Discussed

 Posted on July 2, 2024  |  10 minutes  |  2052 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Ever since Wednesday premiered on Netflix on November 23, 2022, people have been unable to stop talking about it. From cinematography to Jenna Ortega’s performance as the titular character —  the show has been critically acclaimed. 

Wednesday had the second-most pre-release viewers of any Netflix original series on the site, behind The Witcher. It eventually premiered at number one on Netflix in 83 countries. 

With 341.2 million hours seen in its first week of release, the series surpassed the previous record holder Stranger Things 4‘s 335.01 million hours to claim the record for the most hours watched in a week for an English-language Netflix series.

Bio and dating life of Wednesday’s cast

With many eyes on the show, people have also been curious about the cast. Mostly, they have been curious about the members’ bio, age, dating life, and partner.

Here is everything we know about them. 

Jenna Ortega

Ortega was born in the United States on September 27, 2002, making her age 20 as of 2022. She began acting as a kid when she was young, winning praise for her portrayal of little Jane in the comedy-drama Jane the Virgin on The CW. 

Similarly, she was critically acclaimed for her role as Harley Diaz in the Disney Channel series Stuck in the Middle. Currently, Ortega has established herself as a scream queen after appearing in movies like Scream, X, and Studio 666.

Talking about her dating life, the star seems to be single right now. When she and Asher Angel attended a Halloween costume party together in 2018, it gave rise to dating rumors. 

However, she hasn’t mentioned anybody else since. Naturally, many of Wednesday‘s admirers have loved speculating about a prospective romance with her co-star Percy Hynes-White. 

Still, the two haven’t given us any signs of a romantic relationship.

Luis Guzmán

Puerto Rican Actor, Luis Guzmán, was born in Puerto Rico on August 28, 1956. At the time of writing, his age is 66. 

Guzmán’s filmography and bio are very interesting since his nearly 40-year career has included a lot of movies and television shows. He has acted in the films like Out of Sight (1998), The Limey (1999), and Traffic, as well as the Paul Thomas Anderson films Boogie Nights (1997), Magnolia (1999), and Punch-Drunk Love (2002). 

He was nominated for the Independent Spirit Award for Best Supporting Male for his performance in The Limey. On Wednesday, he played the role of the patriarch of the Addams Family, Gomez Addams.

Guzmán is a private persona and has not talked about his personal life. However, it is reported that he is happily married to his wife of 30 years, Angelita Galarza. 

The pair have five children together. Other than this, nothing else is known about Guzmán’s dating life. 

Catherine Zeta-Jones

Welsh actress Catherine Zeta-Jones is well known for her versatility in movies and series. Her well-known works include The Mask of Zorro, Entrapment, The Haunting, Traffic, America’s Sweethearts, and Chicago.

On Wednesday, the 53-year-old plays the role of Morticia Addams. To date, she has won several honors, including an Academy Award, a British Academy Film Award, and a Tony Award. 

She received the CBE designation for her contributions to humanitarian causes and the film industry in 2010.  Zeta-Jones has one of the longest marriages in the Hollywood Industry.

She had an exciting dating life before she met her current husband, Michael Douglas. Zeta-Jones was introduced to the American actor by Danny DeVito at the Deauville American Film Festival in France in August 1998. 

The pair have an age difference of 25 years, but it did not stop them from starting their relationship. They got engaged on December 31, 1999, and married almost a year later.

After Douglas’s divorce was officially finalized on November 18, 2000, they were married at the Plaza Hotel in New York City. The BBC dubbed the high-profile event, which cost an estimated £1.5 million, the “wedding of the year.” 

The couple has two children — a son named Dylan Michael and a daughter named Carys Zeta. Until 2009, the family resided in Bermuda, but since 2016, they have been living in New York. 

Isaac Ordonez

There is not much information on Issac Ordonez since the actor has just entered the entertainment industry. All we know about his bio are his age and career to date.

Ordonez was born on April 15, 2009, in Los Angeles, California, making his age 13 as of 2022. He is well-known for his roles in several films and television shows, including Pugsley Addams in Wednesday and Charles Wallace Double in A Wrinkle in Time.

Fred Armisen

American comedian, actor, writer, producer, and musician, Fred Armisen, has been making strides in the entertainment industry for over 25 years. He co-created and co-starred in the IFC sketch comedy series Portlandia with his partner in comedy, Carrie Brownstein.

Along with Bill Hader and Seth Meyers, he co-created and appears in the mockumentary IFC series Documentary Now! He co-created and co-stars in the Showtime comedy series Moonbase 8 with John C. Reilly and Tim Heidecker. 

In addition to being the regular drummer for the Late Night with Seth Meyers house band, The 8G Band, Armisen launched ThunderAnt.com, a website that showcases the comedic skits produced with Brownstein. 

He is also well known for his stint on the NBC late-night variety and comedy program Saturday Night Live. Armisen has worked on several comedy movies, such as Melvin Goes to Dinner, Deuce Bigalow: European Gigolo, The Ex, and The Dictator.

The Last Man on Earth, Toast of London, 30 Rock, Parks and Recreation, Brooklyn Nine-Nine, New Girl, Broad City, Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt, Difficult People, and Curb Your Enthusiasm are just a few of the TV programs he has been in as a guest star. His recent project was Wednesday, in which he played the role of Fester Addams. 

Armisen’s dating life and history are pretty messy. From 1998 to 2004, he was married to English singer-songwriter Sally Timms. 

Then, in 2009, he married actress Elisabeth Moss only to get divorced two years later in 2011. Moss called their time together “extremely traumatic and awful and horrible.”

She went on to say that the best way to characterize Armisen was, “He’s so great at doing impersonations. But the greatest impersonation he does is that of a normal person.”

After Moss, Armisen dated the artist Alice Lancaster. But once again, their relationship did not work. 

It is reported that after their breakup, Lancaster made a painting dedicated to the actor titled ‘Fred Armisen: Portrait of a Sociopath.’ Armisen is aware of his failed relationships.

He confessed to Howard Stern, “I think I was a terrible husband. I think I’m a terrible boyfriend. I feel bad for everyone I’ve gone out with.”

Armisen’s longest relationship was with actress Natasha Lyonne. The pair began dating in 2014, and they parted ways in 2022. 

Lyonne acknowledged that they had broken up in April 2022. He remarked that the couple still loved one other about as much as two people can love each other, and they were still chatting all the time. 

Riki Lindhome

Erika Lindhome, best known as Riki Lindhome, is an American actress, comedian, and singer. She was born on March 5, 1979, making her age 43 as of 2022. 

She is best recognized for her work with the comedic folk duet Garfunkel and Oates as a vocalist and lyricist. However, she has made small appearances in TV series and movies.

She made her television debut in 2002 with small appearances in Titus and Buffy the Vampire Slayer. She then appeared in guest roles on Gilmore Girls, The Big Bang Theory, and Enlightened. 

Riki Lindhome welcomed her son in March 2022. (Source: Instagram)

Together with Natasha Leggero, she co-created and acted in the Comedy Central period comedy Another Period from 2015 to 2018. She played the role of Kimberly Harris in the Fox animated sitcom Duncanville from 2020 to 2022. 

She portrayed Dr. Valerie Kinbott in the Netflix comedy-horror series Wednesday in 2022. Lindhome is very private and does not share much about her personal life.

So other than her professional career, her bio and dating life are unknown. In March 2022, she welcomed her son but did not reveal who the father r her partner was. 

Gwendoline Christie

Gwendoline Christie was born on October 28, 1978, making her age 44 as of 2022. She is most recognized for her roles as Captain Phasma, a First Order stormtrooper, in Star Wars: The Force Awakens and Star Wars: The Last Jedi.

However, her portrayal of  Brienne of Tarth in the HBO fantasy drama series Game of Thrones was appreciated by most.

She was nominated for the Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Drama Series Primetime Emmy Award in 2019,  Saturn Award for Best Supporting Actress on Television in 2013 and 2019, and the Critics’ Choice Television Award for Best Supporting Actress in a Drama Series in 2019 for it.

Since then, Christie has appeared in the Netflix fantasy shows, The Sandman and Wednesday as Lucifer and Larissa Weems, respectively. 

Talking about her dating life, she has been in a relationship with fashion designer Giles Deacon since early 2013. Before dating, the pair were friends for a while. 

Her partner often designs the dresses Christie wears on the red carpet. Deacon was also behind the iconic look of Christie as Lucifer in The Sandman.

Christina Ricci

American actress Christina Ricci was born on February 12, 1980. Her age is 42 in 2022. 

Ricci’s filmography and bio are quite interesting. Recognized for portraying peculiar roles with a sinister edge, most of Ricci’s work is for indie films. 

However, she has also starred in a number of big office successes. In addition to nominations for a Golden Globe, two Primetime Emmys, and a Screen Actors Guild Award, she has received a number of honors, including a National Board of Review Award, a Satellite Award, and other recognitions.

The actress made her cinematic debut at the young age of nine with Mermaids. However, her breakthrough role came with The Addams Family and its sequel, Addams Family Values, where she featured as Wednesday Addams. 

She became well-known as a “teen idol” after making subsequent appearances in Casper and Now and Then. With The Ice Storm, she transitioned into adult-oriented roles at the age of 17, which led to appearances in movies like Buffalo ’66,  Pecker, The Opposite of Sex, Sleepy Hollow, Monster, Prozac Nation, Pumpkin, Anything Else, Cursed, Penelope, Black Snake Moan, Speed Racer, and The Matrix Resurrections.

On March 21, 2022, it was announced that Ricci had been cast as a series regular in Tim Burton’s Wednesday for Netflix. Marilyn Thornhill, a botany teacher at Nevermore Academy, is played by her.

Ricci’s dating life is like a rollercoaster. In 2008, Ricci began dating comedian and actor Owen Benjamin, whom she met on the filming of the film All’s Faire in Love. 

They were engaged in March 2009 but broke up two months later. Ricci then started dating James Heerdegan, whom she met while shooting on Pan Am in 2012.

The pair confirmed her engagement in February 2013 and married in Manhattan on October 26, 2013. Ricci and Heerdegan were blessed with a baby boy in August 2014.

Ricci filed for divorce on July 2, 2020, from her partner after nearly seven years of marriage. Ricci said in her divorce suit that Heerdegen subjected her to serious physical and emotional abuse.

To make matters worse, she confessed that several of these acts of violence occurred in front of their kid. This was corroborated by previous incidents where police were called to the pair’s house.

On June 25, 2020, the Los Angeles Police Department responded to a report at Ricci and Heerdegen’s Woodland Hills, California residence. Although Heerdegen was not arrested, Ricci received an emergency protective order against Heerdegen the day before she filed for divorce. 

Ricci was awarded a domestic violence restraining order against Heerdegen in January 2021. She was given full custody of their kid in April 2021, but Heerdegen received visiting privileges.

After Heerdegan, Ricci started dating hairdresser Mark Hampton. The duo announced in August 2021 that they were expecting a baby. 

Then, two months later, on October 9, 2021, Ricci announced her marriage to Hampton. They welcomed their baby daughter Cleopatra in December 2021.
