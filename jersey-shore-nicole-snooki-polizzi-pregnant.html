Jersey Shore fans think Nicole Snooki Polizzi dropped new 'clue' shes pregnant in new photo

 Posted on February 13, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  366 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

JERSEY Shore star Nicole Polizzi, also known as Snooki, has posted a new photo that has fans wondering if she's pregnant.

The 35-year-old reality star shared the picture on her Instagram Feed on Wednesday.

The photo showed Nicole holding her adorable one-year-old dog who was looking directly at the camera through his black fur.

The television star was standing in her home right next to her back door while wearing a black and pink floral shirt.

She covered half of her face and her entire stomach with the medium-sized dog as she hugged him.

The Jersey Shore personality captioned the post: "Crying. My 4th baby."

Another joked: "I thought it was your 4th baby? Wow ..." while a third bluntly stated, "She is PREGNANT."

The couple brought Giovanna Marie into the world on September 26, 2014, and Angelo James on May 30, 2019.

The MTV star said: "Even if they're 30, I'm like, I don't want you watching that. It's weird.
