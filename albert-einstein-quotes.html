Top 21 Quotes of Albert Einstein

 Posted on April 1, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  488 words  |  Martina Birk

Albert Einstein, a German-born American physicist (one who studied matter and energy and their interactions), revolutionized the science of physics.

His theory of relativity asserts that space and time measurements depend on the observer’s motion.

Einstein was born in Ulm, Germany, on March 14, 1879, but grew up in Munich. Some teachers assumed he was retarded since he couldn’t talk fluently at age 9. (with ease and grace). He was fascinated by natural rules and marveled by the invisible yet real, force that directed a compass needle. He started playing violin at age 6 and never stopped. At 12, he was intrigued by geometry’s obvious and definite arguments. Einstein learned maths at 16 (a type of higher mathematics used to solve problems in physics and engineering).
