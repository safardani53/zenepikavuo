How old is Melissa Tattam and is the Made In Chelsea lass engaged to Harry Baron?

 Posted on August 10, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  378 words  |  Larita Shotwell

MADE In Chelsea's cast was shaken up the form of Melissa Tattam who arrived on the scene.

But who is the girl who's tamed ladies' man Harry Baron and what's her background? Here's the lowdown...

Who is Melissa Tattam?

Melissa made her debut on the SW3 show, joining her boyfriend, Harry Baron, who signed up to Made in Chelsea mid-way through series 13 of the show, in 2017, as Sam Thompson's mate.

The 21-year-old socialite, who set up MELISSAJAY swimwear in 2016, bagged a host of admirers as she arrives on set.

Melissa met Harry over Christmas and the couple has been besotted ever since.

Fans saw Harry Baron as a ladies' man with a reputation as a troublemaker who likes to flirt with his friends' girlfriends.

However things are going swimmingly for the couple and they've even moved in together.

The pair aren't engaged just yet but they have already discussed the possibility.

What is MELISSAJAY?

MELISSAJAY is a bikini brand founded by Melissa Tatum herself in 2016.

It was launched while Melissa was living in Mallorca and France and struggled to find a bikini that flattered her figure.

The site sells two-piece bikinis, sarongs and shirts.

Bikini prices start at £79.20 for an individual bikini top or bottom and sarongs all sell for £35.00.

Explaining her range on the website, she said: "The bikini top allows you to have as much natural cleavage as possible without all the padding, while the bottoms feature Brazilian-style ruching that keeps its shape even when wet.

"Each piece is crafted with simplicity, style and quality in mind. They’re made with luxury Italian fabrics that stand out on sunkissed skin and feature bespoke prints designed by Melissa herself."
