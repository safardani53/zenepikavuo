Who is richest housewife from the Real Housewives franchise?

 Posted on July 18, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  628 words  |  Larita Shotwell

BRAVO's Real Housewives have lifestyles and wealth that are a world apart from most of our realities.

While the stars all have opulent and luxury lifestyles, there are some housewives in the franchise whose net worth eclipse that of their co-stars.

Many have made their money outside of their television appearances, with their own brands and businesses.

Kandi Buress was a wealthy woman when she joined The Real Housewives of Atlanta, thanks to her prior music career.

Her estimated net worth is around $30 million, stemming from her time as an active part of the music industry as a songwriter and member of the vocal group Xscape, and later as a television personality.

She's been married to Todd Tucker, a former producer of the show, since 2014.

However, even her wealth is eclipsed by the wealthiest Housewife of them all - Lisa Vanderpump.

Together with her husband, Ken Todd, Lisa has built a fortune in the hospitality industry, owning and operating dozens of restaurants, bars, and clubs.

Her businesses put her net worth at a whopping $75 million, says ScreenRant.com.

Tailing behind Lisa as the second richest is Carole Radziwill, a former cast member of The Real Housewives Of New York City.

The former princess, through her marriage to Polish prince Anthony Radziwill, until his death in 1999 - is worth of $50 million.

The successful writer and journalist has published books including What Remains: A Memoir of Fate, and Friendship and Love.

At around the same $50 million net worth mark is RHOBH OG Kyle Richards.

Richards had a career as an actress, starring in Halloween, Little House On The Prairie, and The Watcher In The Woods, before joining the franchise in 2010.

She has been married to successful realtor Mauricio Umansky, since 1996.

Camille Grammer, RHOBH alum, also has a current net worth is estimated to be somewhere around $50 million.

When Frasier star Kelsey Grammer and Camille finalized their divorce, she reportedly received a $30-$50 million settlement including cash and property.

She started her showbiz career as a dancer in the 1980s on Club MTV; the couple share two children.

Her RHOBH co-star Dorit Kemsley also has an estimated net worth of $50 million

Dorit's husband Paul Kemsley is a successful real estate developer and celebrity manager, who currently manages soccer legend Pele and singer Boy George.

Dorit has also made money from her fashion endeavors, including her own brand of swimwear, Dorit.

Tailing Dorit, with an estimated net worth around $45 million, is Yolanda Hadid.

Yolanda was part of the cast of The Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills, where her model daughters, Gigi and Bella Hadid, were also sometimes featured.

She was paid $100,000 per episode.

The former model married property developer Mohamed Hadid in 1994, whom she later went to divorce in 2000.

Divorce documents revealed that she received a settlement that included $3.6 million in cash, a $6 million house in Malibu, a mansion in Santa Barbara, several cars (including her Escalade and Range Rover), and $30,000 in monthly child support.

Jill Zarin from The Real Housewives Of New York is just behind Yolanda, with $40 million net worth.

RHOC star Heather Dubrow - who is married to plastic surgeon Terry Dubrow - had an acting career in Hawaii-Five-O before joining the reality show.

The mother-of-four has a fortune of around $40 million.

RHONY star Tinsley Mortimer only joined the cast in 2017, but quickly established herself as one of the most successful women to ever be part of the franchise.

Mortimer's estimated net worth of $35 million stems not only from her television salary, but from her several business ventures, including several product lines in the areas of homeware, beauty and travel.
