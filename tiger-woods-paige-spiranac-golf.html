 Posted on May 23, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  317 words  |  Aldo Pusey

TIGER WOODS posted a video of himself hitting golf balls for the first time since his horror car crash in February, leaving Paige Spiranac in awe.

There were fears the 15-time major champion may never swing a club again after the accident, which caused multiple injuries to his right leg and foot.

Woods even admitted he was aiming just to be able to walk again unaided.

But, after the legend - who wore a compression sock on his right leg - revealed he is "making progress" in a three-second clip on Twitter, golf beauty Spiranac has tipped him to make a sensational return to tour.

She replied: "He’s winning another major."

Woods won his last major at the Masters in 2019, eleven years after his previous one following a spate of back injuries and inconsistent form.

And PGA Touy pal Justin Thomas thinks Woods is 'going to try' and make his way back onto the professional scene.
