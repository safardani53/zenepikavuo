TRAVIS Kelce is making sure Taylor Swift's wardrobe is hitting all the right notes over the festive period.

The superstar couple spent their first Christmas together since their relationship became public earlier this year.

And a source close to the Kansas City Chiefs star has told The U.S. Sun about some of the elaborate gifts under the Cruel Summer singer's tree.

Kind-hearted Travis, 34, has already booked some sumptuous getaways to help Taylor relax and unwind during the grueling European leg of her next world tour.

The pair plan to spend time together in between sell-out shows in Paris and Milan, soaking up as much culture as possible along the way.

And Taylor, 34, can stroll through the streets wearing some of the gifts her new love carefully, and thoughtfully, purchased for her.

Our insider said the Super Bowl winner picked out some super stylish, Christian Louboutin shoes, which cost around $5,000 after he customized them for Taylor.

He bought her a matching bag, setting him back about $9,000.

The Condora Booty Strass Rainbow shoes match several sparkly outfits the mega-star singer has worn on stage, and according to the Louboutin website, the toe is pointed "like a bird's beak."

"This sophisticated ankle boot in black suede is adorned with hand-placed strass in the shape of a rainbow," the description states.

"It has a 100 mm slanted heel, showcasing Maison Christian Louboutin's innovative creativity. With its sleek lines playing with graphic elements, this model exudes timelessness."

The matching clutch is also embellished with the rainbow effect to add some extra dazzle on a night out.

"The Loubitwist clutch in size small has a twisted shape, showcasing Maison Christian Louboutin's creativity," said the designer's official website.

"Crafted in velvet and adorned with multicolored rainbow strass, this model will add an elegant touch to any urban or evening look."

Taylor was also given a chic, all-occasion jacket from Italian designer Ferragamo, which uses nappa blouson leather, known to be the finest around.

And based on its description, it appears the jacket was ready-made to be rocked by a world-famous artist, on or off stage.

"Sumptuous nappa blouson with clean lines and cool details for an off duty jacket with a rock vibe," says the product details.

"The custom zip along the front leads to a shirt collar. Four zip pockets on the front echo a gritty feel."

And, for the final flourish, Travis snapped up a beautiful, $23,000 Cartier watch.

The 18-karat yellow gold timepiece has a "golden sunray dial, blued-steel sword-shaped hands and sapphire crystal," per Cartier.

"Travis was looking forward to Christmas time as it’s one of his favorite time of the year," said the source.
