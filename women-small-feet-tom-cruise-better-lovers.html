Women think best lovers have unique feature like Tom Cruise - do you have it?

 Posted on May 19, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  220 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

WOMEN reckon blokes with small feet are better lovers.

Despite the myth that men with larger plates of meat are better endowed, it seems those who take a size seven come out on top between the sheets, a survey found.

Some 4,500 women were asked to reveal the shoe size of their greatest lovers — and 23 per cent said size seven measured up best.

In fact, the study by website OnBuy.com found 53 per cent of lasses said lovers with smaller feet — up to a size eight — were the best in bed.

Size 13 and over only got a two per cent vote, with size 12s on four per cent, 11s on 12 per cent and tens on nine per cent.

The average shoe size of UK men is a nine, which came second in the poll with 20 per cent, followed by size eight at 19 per cent.

It’s good news for the likes of ­Hollywood legend Tom Cruise, 60, who takes an eight.

But Scottish actor Gerard Butler, 53, who has size 13 feet, and action hero Arnold Schwarzenegger, 75, a 12, have less to smile about.

Gilly Haddow, 36, from St Albans, Herts, said of an ex- lover with size 12 feet: “I’d heard the rumours and they were just that — rumours.”
