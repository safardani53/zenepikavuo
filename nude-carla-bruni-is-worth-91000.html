Who says you can’t put a price on beauty?

A nude photo of French first lady Carla Bruni sold for $91,000 last week. The shot of President Nicolas Sarkozy’s wife was taken by photographer Michel Comte 15 years ago.

A buyer for a Chinese art collector purchased the black-and-white 1993 photo and, we can only assume, plans to display it next to the most scantily clad Michelle Obama picture he can find.
