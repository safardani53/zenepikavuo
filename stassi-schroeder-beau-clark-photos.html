Stassi Schroeder and Beau Clark have been married since 2020. See pics of them here.

Stassi Schroeder went through quite a few public relationships before she met her lifetime love, Beau Clark, a casting agent. ‘Vanderpump Rules’ fans watched her messy relationships with Jax Taylor, Frank Herlihy and Patrick Meagher before she introduced Beau to the world on season 7 of the hit Bravo series. Stassi and Beau began dating in February 2018, just six months after Meagher dumped her on their four-year anniversary. Beau has become a fan favorite on ‘Vanderpump Rules’ and quickly became close with cast. Stassi confirmed Beau was joining the cast of ‘Vanderpump Rules’ in Octobet 2018, ahead of show’s season 7 premiere. ‘Baby’s first credits #pumprules,’ she captioned a photo of the couple from the set of the opening credits. He proposed to Stassi on July 31, 2019, and they got married in a small ceremony in Sept. 2020. They later welcomed their first child together and had a big wedding in Italy in May 2022. Their second child was born in September 2023.
