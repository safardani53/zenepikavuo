Who is Ben Shapiro's sister Abigail?

Abigail Shapiro is the sister of controversial political commentator Ben Shapiro.

He is the founder and editor-in-chief of The Daily Wire, an American conservative news website.

Abigail was born on June 2000 Tampa, Florida and is 21-years-old.

She is a social media influencer, digital creator, YouTuber, actress and stuntwoman.

Her Instagram account named Classically Abby boasts more than 54k followers where she regularly posts snaps online.  

She describes herself as “a wife, mama-to-be, an opera singer, an entrepreneur, a YouTuber, and your guide to becoming the classic woman you’ve always wanted to be.”

At the time of writing, the influencer is expecting her first child with her husband Jacob who are sharing their journey across social media.  

In a post on October 6, 2021, she wrote: “We are officially 16 weeks along! I can't wait to share the whole process with you - what the first trimester was like, what I'm experiencing now, what we are planning for the future, and so much more.”

Do Ben and Abigail have any other siblings?

The duo have another sister named Milly Shapiro who is an actress and stuntwoman.

The siblings hail from an Orthodox Jewish family and were exposed to music at a young age.

Abigail is particularly popular among her following for her singing skills and revealed her brother played violin when he was 12.

Her website notes that after finishing college at the University of Southern California and the Manhattan School of Music, she found herself at a loss as she “left behind” her traditional values.

Who are their parents?

Abigail’s parents are Eri and Michelle Shapiro.

Michelle, Abigail and Milly all suffer from cleidocranial dysostosis – a birth defect that mostly affects the bones and teeth.
