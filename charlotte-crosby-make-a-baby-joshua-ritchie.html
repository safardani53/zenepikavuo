CHARLOTTE Crosby has made it no secret in the past that she wants to have a baby.

And the 29-year-old took to Instagram last night to tell fans that she was going to make one as she lay in bed naked with her boyfriend, Joshua Ritchie.

Charlotte - who is currently on holiday with the former Love Island star- began by teasing her followers as she said that she had two surprise announcements.

"All I can say is November is going to be a fun month," she said.

She added: "They're both just as exciting as the other.

"No one is having a baby before you all jump to the conclusion."

Josh, 25,  jokingly chimed in: "We'll be expecting it in nine months time, after tonight! Let's get making a baby."

Charlotte replied: "OK then Josh, come on then. Shall we do it?

He quipped: "No, you're on a sex ban for at least three years."

But Charlotte ended the clip, as she revealed: "No come on I think I'm ovulating. Right, we've got to go, we've got to make some babies."

This isn't the first time that Charlotte has hinted about 'babies' in recent weeks, as she shared a snap with Josh ahead of her move to Bolton last week.

Wearing a blouse, white trousers and tousled hair, Charlotte look smitten as she gazed at the former Love Island star, who opted for a casual look in a black zip jacket, white t-shirt and jeans.

She wrote: "Cheers to the next chapter. Celebrating the fact we get the keys to our little home together in 2 days.

"CANT BELIEVE IM GUNNA LIVE WITH A BOY."

Reposting the snap on to her Story, Charlotte went on to add: "NEXT STOP BABIESSSSS."

The gushing post didn't go amiss with Josh's mum, Karen, who further fuelled speculation that the reality star is expecting.

She wrote on the snap: "My future grandchild right there @charlottegshore @joshuaritchie1 there we go number 10."

In another comment, Karen added: "Cannot wait here's we're the fun starts. It will be perfect".

Charlotte, who suffered from an ectopic pregnancy in 2016, has always been vocal when it comes to starting a family with Josh.

In January, the star revealed her plans to get pregnant next year while appearing on Good Morning Britain.

Talking to host Ben Shepherd, she said: "Next year! Me and Josh.

"I would like to yes, I'm 29 this year so I'll be 30 next year and I'm really ready to start a family."
