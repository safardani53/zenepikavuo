Who is Gemma Owen and who is she dating?

 Posted on July 21, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  556 words  |  Martina Birk

FORMER Love Island star Gemma Owen has wasted no time on getting back out there after her split with Luca Bish.

Just eight weeks after her break up with the fishmonger, it has been revealed that she is now dating rapper Bugzy Malone.

Who is Gemma Owen?

Gemma Owen is a 19-year-old Team GB dressage competitor, who owns a swimwear company.

She is the eldest of former England and Liverpool football player Michael Owen and childhood sweetheart Louise Bonsall's four children.

The young star launched her swimwear company - OG Beachwear - during the first coronavirus lockdown back in 2020.

Gemma is an international dressage rider and represented Great Britain at the European Championships in July 2021.

She came second place on Love Island 2022 with her now ex-boyfriend Luca Bish.

Since leaving the villa, she has signed a six-figure brand deal with PLT and released her own jewellery range.

Who is Gemma Owen dating? 

Gemma is dating rapper Bugzy Malone - whose real name is Aaron Davies.

A pal of the 19-year-old told The Sun: “Gemma and Aaron have enjoyed a few dates over the past few weeks.

“They have told their closest friends they are dating and seem really happy together.

“Gemma and Aaron are keeping their relationship out of the public eye for now because it is early days.

“He is really into her and has made it clear how he feels.

“It is all very new and exciting for both of them but they are taking it slowly.”

Bugzy, 32, is known as one of the first Manchester-born grime stars to see mainstream success in the UK.

His album B. Inspired went on to peak at No. 6 in the UK charts, while his biggest hits included Kilos featuring Aitch.

Why did Gemma Owen split up with Luca Bish?

The influencer and Luca Bish separated in November 2022, only three months after they left Love Island.

Gemma shared a statement to Instagram confirming their split, while Luca shared his own post hours later stating that he wished he'd had time to “process the break-up privately”.

A source told the MailOnline that the former couple's romance always “had cracks” and that they failed to solve their issues.

The source said: “In no way was Luca blindsided, they'd been on the rocks for quite a few weeks. They'd nearly split up quite a few times before this.

“Cracks had been forming the past few weeks, they brushed it over. They tried to go to Dubai to see if it would help but things didn't change.” 

Who else has Gemma Owen dated?

Gemma had a relationship with rugby player and fellow islander Jacques O'Neill before entering Love Island.

The couple called time on their relationship at the beginning of 2021 - but they were reunited again when Jacques entered the ITV2 show in 2022.

During their time in the villa Gemma and Jacques remained friends despite a few awkward moments.

The 19-year-old has also reportedly dated ex-Wrexham footballer Cameron Green.
