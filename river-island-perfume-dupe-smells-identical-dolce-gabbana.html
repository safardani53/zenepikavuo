Bargain hunters race to nab River Island perfume dupe that smells 'identical to Dolce &amp; Gabbana' &amp; i

 Posted on February 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  424 words  |  Aldo Pusey

BARGAIN hunters are racing to the high street, desperate to get their hands on a perfume from River Island.

So if you’re a beauty fan and want to smell great whilst on a budget, you’ll need to listen up.

Many shoppers will have hit the shopping malls already to check out the epic Boxing Day sales - but if you’re a perfume lover, you’ll need to pop into River Island.

Especially if you’re looking to save cash, the popular chain is selling a dupe perfume that’s ‘identical’ to an iconic Dolce & Gabbana scent.

One shopper managed to get her hands on the affordable buy, which was reduced in the sale, and was left thrilled with her bargain.

Amy Sherwood took to social media to show off her discounted perfume, as she posted on Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK, a private Facebook group with a staggering 2.3 million members.

Amy shared a screenshot of the perfume from River Island’s website and beamed: “Bargain!”

She then claimed: “Identical to Dolce & Gabbana Devotion and lasts ages.”

The savvy shopper later confirmed that she spotted the spray “in Meadowhall, Sheffield.”

Originally priced at £16 for an 100ml bottle of Amber & Vanilla, the spray had a £6 reduction, and was “reduced to £10 in the sale.”

According to River Island, you can ‘add a touch of spice to your senses with this comforting, bold fragrance.’

Infused with notes of rich pepper, amber and vanilla, this perfume is certainly not one to be missed, and if you’re a fan of Dolce & Gabbana’s Devotion, you’ll want to give this a try. 

However, the designer eau de parfum is sold for an eye-watering £124 from John Lewis for an 100ml bottle.

So if you fancy having an extra £114 in your pocket, whilst still smelling seductive and reassuring, you’ll need to run to the high street. 

Amy shared her post just 16 hours ago, but it has clearly impressed many, as it has quickly caught the attention of many bargain hunters.  

Social media users were desperate to get their hands on the dupe buy and many were keen to express this in the comments. 

One person said: “Off there to get this.” 

Another added: “Love this.” 

A third commented: “My daughter has this, it smells so nice.”
