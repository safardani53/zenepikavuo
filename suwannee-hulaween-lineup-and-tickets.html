Suwannee Hulaween 2022: Lineup and where to buy tickets?

 Posted on May 20, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  423 words  |  Larita Shotwell

THE SUWANNEE Hulaween festival is back for the second time since 2019.

The festival did not take place in 2020 due to the Covid-19 pandemic but returned in 2021 and will be back in 2022.

When is the Suwannee Hulaween festival?

The Suwannee Hulaween festival kicks off in Live Oak, Florida on October 27, 2022, and runs through October 30 for Halloween.

Visitors will have access to food, drinks, and merchandise vendors which newly accept debit and credit cards in addition to cash sales.

Those heading to the festival for music and fun will find it hidden in the moss-covered area of North Florida.

The festival was announced on May 20, and pre-sale tickets were released on May 23 and sold out by the end of the day.

How can I buy tickets?

Pre-sale tickets to Suwannee Hulaween have sold out but general admission tickets are still available on a limited, first come first serve basis.

General admission tickets can be purchased for either a 3-day pass for $324 or a 4-day pass for $404. A payment plan option is available when purchasing tickets on the festival's website.

The two bottom tier VIP packages have sold out, however, the top four tiers are still available, starting at $3,699 for the Witch VIP glamping package.

The Goblin package for a VIP RV for two is available for $3,099 and the Vampire package for a VIP RV for four is available for $4,999.
