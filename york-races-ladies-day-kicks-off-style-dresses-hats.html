York Races Ladies Day kicks off in style with colour-popping dresses and show-stopping hats

 Posted on June 25, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  799 words  |  Larita Shotwell

IT’S LADIES' Day at York Races today and glamorous racegoers have pulled out all the stops.

From eye-popping dresses to towering stilettos, Ebor Festival revellers have not held back with their stylish ensembles.

Today is Day Two of the Sky Bet Ebor Festival, which is running from Wednesday to Saturday at the famous racecourse.

Guests could be seen arriving in high spirits for a day of racing in the sun with their friends and family.

And they certainly have gone all out with their wardrobe choices, with unmissable fascinators and colourful dresses being the order of the day.

For those who feel they’ve truly nailed their looks, the Ebor Fashion Lawn once again takes place on all four days of the festival.

The website states: “Combining fashion and fun, the catwalk is open to all and offers a chance to show off your style whilst friends cheer from the front row. 

“It is part of the flagship Festival at York Racecourse, the week to visit featuring style as well as the oldest, richest, fastest and most famous races.”

A judging panel is on hand to witness the most stylish racegoers each day of the festival and hand out prizes.

These will go to Most Stylish, Most Stylish Couple, Most Stylish Runner-Up, Best Hat and Best Dressed Child.

The exciting festival has no set dress code, but women are encouraged to "sport dresses, high heeled shoes together with hats or fascinators".

Guests are also reminded to dress "within the bounds of decency" and take inspiration from "wedding attire".

The racecourse website does add that "it’s most important that you feel comfortable and enjoy your day".
