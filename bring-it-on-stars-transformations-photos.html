‘Bring It On’ is one of the millennial generation’s favorite movies, and for good reason! The witty film, which stars Kirsten Dunst and Gabrielle Union, tackles ideas of appropriation in the confines of high school cheerleading culture. Two rival schools go at it, but one of them has been stealing their best cheers from the other for years! What ensues is total hilarity and lots of spirit fingers. 

In the 20 years since the film hit theaters, the cast has accomplished so much. Leading stars Gabrielle Union and Kirsten Dunst have cultivated prosperous careers and become two of the most respected women in Hollywood — taking on independent projects, and using their platforms for social causes. So let’s take a look back at this cast!
