What Happened to YoungBoy Never Broke Again's Instagram Profile?

 Posted on July 17, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  588 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

What happened to YoungBoy Never Broke Again's Instagram? The "Valuable Pain" rapper deleted his page a while ago. Read on to find out why.

In April 2020, YoungBoy Never Broke Again (aka Kentrell DeSean Gaulden) deleted every photograph posted on his Instagram account, profile picture included. According to some fans, the rapper did so in a bid to curb the number of damning comments directed against him. So, what happened?

YoungBoy Never Broke Again wiped his entire Instagram account clean in April 2020.

"You know why I deleted my pictures off my page? [...] Women trying to incriminate me. Posting stupid shit. Well, they say stupid shit. I guess the people that they down talk a n--ga with leaked the shit. I don't know. That shit be playing mind games. Well, I be playing mind games with myself because I be letting it eat me up," YoungBoy Never Broke Again explained in a previous Instagram Live with fellow Baton Rouge-native Bway Yungy.

As the rapper revealed in the Instagram Live, he deleted his posts in a bid to clear up his reputation and avoid having to deal with less than favorable comments. 

YoungBoy Never Broke Again had a heated comment exchange with Kodak Black as well.

Others believe that YoungBoy Never Broke Again made up his mind after getting caught up in a heated exchange with Kodak Black, who lambasted him after learning about Iyanna Mayweather's arrest. 

According to outlets like SOHH and The Blast, the "Dying to Live" rapper took it to the social media platform sometime around early April to allege that YoungBoy Never Broke Again cooperated with the police during Mayweather's arrest.

It's understood that Kodak shared a picture of a caption originally posted by gossipofthecity_ on his Instagram page. In the caption, he condemned YoungBoy Never Broke Again for his supposed cooperation with the police.

"Watch me from a jail cell, man. [...] N---a b----es – ain't never paid attention to a n---a especially from no mother---ing cell, n---a. Not her b---h a-- daddy?" the rapper clapped back in a now-deleted Instagram Live.

Iyanna Mayweather was arrested for felony aggravated assault with a deadly weapon.

Mayweather was arrested on Saturday, April 4, 2020, for felony aggravated assault with a deadly weapon after reportedly stabbing YoungBoy Never Broke Again's baby mama, Lapattra Lashai Jacobs, in the bicep several times.

The incident took place at the rapper's Houston home, a previous article by TMZ reveals. Mayweather was taken to jail in Harris County, Tex., at around 1.30 a.m., as per NBC News. It's uncertain whether YoungBoy Never Broke Again would have cooperated with the police like the allegations hold. 

To make matters even more complicated, the rapper's Instagram account has been mysteriously deleted since then.
