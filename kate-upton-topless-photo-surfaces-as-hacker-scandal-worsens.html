Kate Upton Topless Photo Surfaces as Hacker Scandal Worsens

 Posted on April 16, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  217 words  |  Martina Birk

Kate Upton is the latest victim of a hacker who first received notice for posting nude photos of Jennifer Lawrence online. 

Following the Lawrence scandal, the hacker posted naked pics of Victoria Justice amidst rumors that he was targeting starlets such as Ariana Grande and Kate Upton next.

That rumor has now been partially confirmed, as a topless selfie of Upton was posted to the photo sharing site Imgur just moments after the Justice photos surfaced.

Fortunately for Upton, the hacker only posted one photo of her, and it’s not quite as revealing as the photos of Lawrence and Justice.

Lawrence has yet to comment publicly, but Justice has already taken to Twitter to claim that the photos that supposedly show her naked are not authentic:

 

“These so called nudes of me are FAKE people. Let me nip this in the bud right now *pun intended*”

Other stars who were involved in the scandal such as actress Mary Elizabeth Winstead have confirmed that their photos are real and expressed hopes that the person responsible is brought to justice.

Lawrence has reportedly already contacted the police, and an investigation has been launched in an effort to determine the identity of the hacker. 

We’ll keep you posted on this developing story.
