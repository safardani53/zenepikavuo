The Marked Heart Season 2 Is Set To Premiere On Netflix

 Posted on February 5, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  499 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Season 2 of the Colombian thriller drama The Marked Heart will be published on Netflix globally on Wednesday, April 19, 2023, at 3:01 a.m. ET. The program was originally called Pálpito in Spanish. It is an adrenaline-pumping movie that provides spectators with a peek at the horrible organ trafficking business. Thousands of people are killed each year as a result of this unlawful trade.

The Marked Heart stars, Ana Lucía Domínguez as Camila Duarte, Michel Brown as Simón Duque, Sebastián Martínez as Zacarías Cienfuegos, Moisés Arizmendi as Mariachi, Valeria Emiliani as Samantha Duque, Margarita Muñoz as Valeria Duque, Julián Cerati as Tomás, and Mauricio Cujar as Braulio Cárdenas, Juan Fernando Sánchez as Juan Carlos Sarmiento.

Season 2 of The Marked Heart is now available on Netflix.

In season one of The Marked Heart, Simón Duque’s wife Valeria was killed by a criminal syndicate in order to transplant her heart to another person. Camila, the wife of a rich guy, was the lucky receiver of the heart. Camila had no idea where her new heart had come from. Meanwhile, a bereaved Simon is determined to revenge his wife’s death. Simon and Camila gradually met and fell in love. Both characters found the truth at the conclusion.

According to the above teaser, Camila and Simón are in a whole new realm of turmoil. Despite the fact that she was married to a rich man called Zacaras Cienfuegos, who had saved her life, she fell in love with Simone. She held the heart of Simón’s late wife, Valeria. Camila had pretended to die in order to flee from her wicked husband’s life, but Zacaras had prepared a grand scheme to bring her back and get vengeance on Simón. He’s determined to teach Simón a lesson.

Season 1 has fourteen episodes. All of them were published on April 20, 2022. Following its immediate popularity, the program was immediately renewed for a second season. In an interview in April 2022, showrunner Leonardo Padrón revealed that he intended to portray how a person’s psyche reacts when a foreign item (in this instance, a heart) is brought into their body via Camila’s character. Camila starts to feel like a different person in the performance as she sees Valeria in her visions. She even develops affection for Simón, who is likewise irresistibly drawn to her. Padrón, who is also a screenwriter and executive producer on the program, secured an exclusive three-year creative pact with Netflix last year for the creation of new episodes and films.

Synopsis and additional information about The Marked Heart

According to IMDb, the summary of Leonardo Padrón’s Marked Heart says,

“Simón’s wife was murdered in order to extract her heart and transplant it into Camila, the wealthy man’s wife.” In quest of vengeance, he enters the perilous world of organ trafficking.”

Camilo Vega is the show’s only director, while Diego Jiménez is the cinematographer. Each episode lasts 38 to 51 minutes. CMO Producciones is the official production firm.
