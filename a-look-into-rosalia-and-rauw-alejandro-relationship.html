A Look Into Rosalia And Rauw Alejandro Relationship

 Posted on August 9, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  529 words  |  Martina Birk

Rosalia, the singer, recently revealed her marriage to partner Rauw Alejandro. She revealed the news in a music video for the tune Beso, in which she held a ruby ring case. Fans got a peek at her engagement band as she was seen with emotions in her eyes. Rosalia and Rauw have been in a relationship since 2021.

The singer also shared the occasion on Instagram, writing in English, ”

“RR is finally out after more than three years in the making, and here are these three songs made with all the love in the world.” We hope you enjoy this small taste of us.”

On March 13, 2023, the duo revealed their joint endeavor, RR. They announced at the moment that RR would be published on March 24th, and the post highlighted the project’s cover, which featured two hand-painted Rs entwined on a red backdrop.

In 2022, Rosalia and Rauw Alejandro enjoyed their first Valentine’s Day together.

Rosalia and Rauw Alejandro were seen out for supper in Los Angeles in August 2021, sparking speculation that they were courting. The following month, they verified their relationship on TikTok, where Rosalia shared a video of herself and Alejandro forming a heart shape with their limbs. According to Harper’s Bazaar, the pair will make their red carpet premiere at the Los40 Music Awards in Palma de Mallorca in November 2021. On February 14, 2022, they enjoyed their first Valentine’s Day together. Rauw posted a few photos from the couple’s wedding day to his Instagram account. Rauw received a tattoo of his fiancée the following month. In September 2022, the couple will commemorate Rosalia’s birthday in New York City.

On November 17, 2022, the couple was observed at the 23rd Annual Latin Grammy Awards, where the Pain and Glory singer received the Album of the Year honor. In an interview with Rolling Stone, Alejandro stated that his partner had a significant influence on his music and influenced him in various ways. Alejandro noted Rosalia’s assistance with his songs to Zane Lowe for Apple songs and stated:

“Thanks to my girl, I’ve been taking vocal lessons.” ‘You need to exercise more,’ she always said. Okay, I’ll take care of it. I’ll take care of it. Every day, she works on her voice with her tutor.”

In an interview with Entertainment Tonight, he also voiced his “proudness” for her, saying:

“I’m very pleased with you, buddy. She’s incredible. She merits everything that has occurred and is occurring in her life. She works extremely hard, and, you know, [I’m] a very contented guy.

Rauw Alejandro’s records and songs are well-known.

In his flourishing singing career, he has published three CDs under the name Raul Alejandro Ocasio Ruiz.  Afrodisiaco, his second record, was published in November 2020 and peaked at 75th on the US Billboard 200. This was followed in 2021 by Vice Versa, which featured 14 songs and peaked at number 17 on the US Billboard 200. Saturno, his most recent record, was published in November 2022 and peaked at number 25 on the US Billboard 200. He has 80 songs to his credit, including Que Le De, Mirame, Detective, Una Noche, Tattoo, TBT, Elegi, and others.
