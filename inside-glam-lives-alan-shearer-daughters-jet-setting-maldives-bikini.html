Inside the VERY glam lives of Alan Shearers daughters, from jet-setting to the Maldives and bikin

 Posted on April 12, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  673 words  |  Martina Birk

ALAN Shearer may be a household sporting name - but his two very glamorous daughters are taking Instagram by storm and have their own dedicated fanbase.

Hollie, 27, and Chloe, 31, Shearer have over 66,000 followers between them, and regularly show off their globe-trotting adventures and very stylish snaps.

Jet-setting lifestyle

Hollie has racked up a legion of fans with her travel snaps on Instagram, which include hammock shots from the Maldives and beach days in Portugal.

The blonde bombshell doesn’t shy away from swimwear snaps, and recently posed in a skimpy blue bikini wearing a snorkel.

Chloe has also enjoyed a number of very glam trips, including recent breaks to Ibiza, Croatia and Meribel.

Footballer romance

Hollie appears to have confirmed her romance with England rugby star Joe Marchant after sharing more pics of the couple online.

Alan's middle child posted numerous shots with the popular Harlequins centre, who first appeared on her Instagram during a trip to Dubai in March.

Marchant, 25, who has played for England 11 times since his 2019 debut, can be seen wrapping his arms around Hollie on a yacht.

In another picture, the pair can be seen drinking with friends in front of the iconic Jumeirah Hotel.

Enviable wardrobe

When the girls aren’t jetting around the globe to some of the world's most luxurious locations, they regularly look dressed to the nines.

Hollie has done a number of shoots with PrettyLittleThing, and she posed with her sister at Christmas with matching Nadine Merabi feather pyjamas.

They love to dress up when hitting the town, and Hollie recently shared a pic of her posing on a busy London street, wearing an eye-popping pink dress.

Music career

While Chloe is working as a sports agent and has been involved with many BBC productions such as Sport Relief and 606, Hollie works as a singer.

Hollie manages to pull in 15,000 monthly listeners on Spotify, and she cites country music as her biggest inspiration.

Her music career comes after she secured a place at music school in London in 2013.

Fellow Geordies Ant and Dec reached out to the Newcastle legend's daughter in support of her recent single.

On her song Love You Better, Dec said on Instagram: "Love it! Nice work Hol! Dx."

Dragons Den star Peter Jones and Scarlett Moffatt are also fans.

Her other songs include Thank You, Leave Right Now, Sauvignon Blanc and 1400 Days.

Lookalike mum

Shearer fans were left shocked after seeing a photo of Alan’s youthful wife Lainya next to Chloe and Hollie.

Many people mistook the 52-year-old mum-of-three for being sisters with Hollie, 27, and Chloe, 31.

Musician and model Hollie shared a selfie with her glamorous mum and sister, and wrote: “Girls Night.”

And Chloe has also shared snaps of the three of them together, including a stylish Christmas Day show in feather-trimmed pyjamas with the caption: “Spruced Up.”

Unsurprisingly, many people were quick to comment about age-defying Lainya.

One said: “Thought you were all sisters there, you all look amazing.”

Another told Chloe: “You are her double! I can see where you get your beauty from x.”

And a third wrote: “3 sisters yeah?”

Tight family unit

Footballing legend Alan scored a record 260 Premier League goals during his incredible career - but perhaps his beautiful family is his greatest achievement to date.

Alan and Lainya met while he was still playing for Southampton, and they tied the knot a few years later in a private wedding ceremony on June 8, 1991.
