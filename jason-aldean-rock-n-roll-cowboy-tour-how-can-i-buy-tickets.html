Jason Aldean Rock n' Roll Cowboy tour: How can I buy tickets?

 Posted on June 8, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  524 words  |  Larita Shotwell

COUNTRY music star Jason Aldean announced his summer 2022 tour.

It's called the Rock N' Roll Cowboy tour, and we have all the info on tickets and venues.

If you click on a link in this story we may earn affiliate revenue.

How do I buy tickets to Jason Aldean's Rock n' Roll Cowboy tour?

You can buy tickets on Jason Aldean's website, as well as on  Ticketmaster, StubHub, and Vividseats.

Tickets have been on sale since February.

When is Jason Aldean's Rock n' Roll Cowboy tour?

The 2022 tour kicks off in July in Scranton, Pennsylvania.

From there, Aldean will go on a 34 city streak, wrapping up the tour in Wichita, Kansas in late October.

“I’m ready to get back out and feel the nights come alive,” Aldean said on his website.

“We are already thinking about the setlist…there will be some songs off the new record like ‘Rock And Roll Cowboy,’ which felt like a great tour name because it’s all about knowing you belong on the road.”
