Though they had short-lived feud in 2015, Taylor Swift and Nicki Minaj have become close friends in the music world. Look back on their friendship here!

Taylor Swift and Nicki Minaj clearly have the utmost respect for one another! The two women are some of the biggest names in the music industry, and they’ve continued to impress fans album after album. While they come from different genres, it’s clear that they each enjoy one another’s music. Taylor can get into Nicki’s rap, and the “Super Bass” rapper can get down to the Midnights singer’s country and pop music.

Despite their close friendship now, the two of them were actually first connected over a feud, stemming from the 2015 MTV VMAs. They made up and have come quite a long way. Most recently, Nicki gave the “All Too Well” singer a sweet shoutout on X (formerly Twitter).
