Kylie Jenner Celebrating 18th Birthday at Canadian Beach Club: Details

 Posted on March 20, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  459 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Legal to party… in Canada! Kylie Jenner is celebrating her upcoming 18th birthday with a massive party at a beach club in Montreal, Canada, the venue confirmed on its website on Thursday, July 29.

Beachclub Montreal blasted a promotional poster from the event, featuring Kris Jenner’s youngest daughter lounging by the pool in a black bikini, polarized aviators, and distressed denim.

“Kylie Jenner takes advantage of Canadian drinking age with plans to celebrate her birthday in style at Beachclub,” the club owner wrote on his website. “Team Productions and Beachclub’s owner Olivier Primeau stepped up to the plate to offer Kylie the opportunity to party, and drink legally at a fantastic one of a kind venue! The event will take place on August 16th 2015, and will be sure to offer many special guests and great surprises.”

According to Primeau’s site, Beachclub Montreal is the largest outdoor club in the entirety of North America. It includes cabanas, pools, terraces, luxury beds, multiple bars, a mega stage for DJs, and VIP sections. Tickets for the bash will go on sale on Thursday, July 30, at 10 a.m.

TMZ reported on Thursday that Primeau will chopper the birthday girl in for her big event. She will supposedly land on a luxury boat, and celebrate her 18th birthday with a $3,000 cake and plenty of booze.

Just last week, the teen celebrated her high school graduation with a surprise party thrown by Ryan Seacrest and Kris Jenner. “I love my mom for doing this. Thank you for making this experience as normal as possible for me,” Kylie wrote in a message to the Kardashian matriarch. “Yay, I graduated :),” she added.

Meanwhile, her big sister Khloe Kardashian defended her antics — including the #KylieJennerLipChallenge and her relationship with 25-year-old rapper Tyga — in a lengthy interview with Complex.
