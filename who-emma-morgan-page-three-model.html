Who is Emma Morgan and where is the Page Three model now? The Sun

 Posted on June 11, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  199 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

PAGE 3 legend Emma Morgan is considered an icon in the glamour modelling world.

The former model now owns her own business and boasts an illustrious acting career.

Who is Emma Morgan?

Emma Morgan is best known for frequently appearing as a Page 3 model for The Sun throughout the 1990s.

She worked as a glamour model for 12 years, before pursuing an acting career.

Emma has appeared in soaps such as Coronation Street, Hollyoaks, and Emmerdale.

She played the lead role, Jen, in TV drama Guilty Pleasures.

Morgan also plays the lead role, Chloe, in black comedy show Men+al.

Emma has appeared in various pantomimes and stage shows.

Where is Emma Morgan now?

Aside from her acting career, Emma is also the owner of a beautician chain called Belladonna Aesthetics.

She has branches in Manchester, London, and Essex.

Her company does filler and botox.

Is Emma Morgan married?

Emma Morgan is not married, and it is not clear from her social media whether she is in a relationship or not.

However, Morgan does have three children who she frequently posts pictures of on her Instagram.

Their names are Sonny, Flynn, and Olivia.
